About Tyler

Tyler Bettis is a 33-year-old Tennessean who has lived in the state his entire life. Calm, laid-back, thoughtful, but also spontaneous.

He has a creative side—art has always been important. One of his proudest moments was earning first place at his 8th-grade art award ceremony.

Tyler values laughter, friendships, and shared memories, and is known for his dad jokes.

“You should never give up on what you are trying to accomplish.”
